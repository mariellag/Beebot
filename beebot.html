Introducing The Beebot!
A "Robotic Bee" that pollinates your plants.
The Beebot is an easy to use robotic device that can pollinate your plants more efficiently than bees. The Beebot has been shown to increase crop yields and decrease waste, making it the ultimate gardening device.
